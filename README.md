 🌍 EarthWell

**Sustainable living companion for any city.**

EarthWell is a web application that generates instant, city-specific sustainability guides. Users enter any city name and choose Travel or Move mode to receive personalized recommendations on eco-friendly transport, recycling systems, carbon impact, and air quality insights.

---

✨ Features

- **Travel Mode** – Quick sustainability tips for short-term visitors (3–7 days)
- **Move Mode** – In-depth guidance for people relocating to a new city
- **Global Coverage** – Works for any city using AI-generated insights
- **Carbon Estimates** – Approximate CO₂ savings from sustainable choices
- **Air Quality Insights** – City-level environmental awareness
- **Smart Caching** – Faster responses for repeated queries
- **AI Fallback System** – Ensures reliability even when one model fails

---

| Layer | Technology |
